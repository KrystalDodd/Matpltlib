This is based on a study a Mice with Cancer. The data was contained on two separate sheets that were joined with the duplicate Mouse ID and Timepoint removed. 
The clean data was analyzed based on various statistical analysis. 

Bar and pie charts were created to visualize the data. 

The box plots were created to determine the quartiles and outliers.

The data was then placed into a scatterplot to determine the correlation.
